Hi Serhii,

Thanks for the details. That error comes from Polymarket, not from Cosmos. It means Polymarket is rejecting the wallet the order is placed for. Two things to check:

1. Funder address. Your Funder must be your Polymarket deposit (proxy) address, the 0x address shown in the Polymarket app under Deposit. It is different from the wallet address of your private key. If your Funder is the same address as your key, that is the cause. Please set it to your Polymarket deposit address.

2. Deposit flow. Please make sure you added your USDC using the Deposit button inside the Polymarket app, not by sending USDC directly to your wallet. The Deposit step creates the trading wallet that Polymarket requires.

To confirm which one it is, please send me the line from your bot startup log that begins with:
[polymarket] account type:

Once the Funder is your Polymarket deposit address and the deposit is completed, redeploy and it will trade normally.
